About me

My name is Alla

For the last couple of years, I have been into interior design and 3D visualization, but it seemed to me that I was not fully realizing my potential and I wanted something more. After some thought, I decided that front-end development perfectly matches my interests and skills. I love creating beautiful and functional web pages, as well as solving complex problems related to code. I like that this process is constantly changing and developing, and there is always an opportunity to learn new things.

I read a lot of books, blogs, and also take courses on online platforms such as Yandex.Practice, RS School and Hekslet. I also try to attend relevant events in order to communicate with colleagues and find new ideas.

My favorite hobby is cycling. I love spending time in nature, riding my bike through picturesque places. I am also into photography. I love to capture moments of life and save them in beautiful and original frames.
